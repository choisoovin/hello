Self-portrait_Silvergelatinfiberprint_by Soovin Choi(2018)



Soovin LOVES working at the


darkroom.


Most of her works are black and white photographs printed in darkroom:silver gelatin print.
She likes to explore all kinds of different alternative processes as well.
Cyanotypes, Van Dyke, digital transfer, and gel medium transfer. Most of them are used in 19th century.
Does film photography with 35mm, medium formats, and slide film. Currently, she does digital works.
Her experience in photography would enrich her digital media artwork.
Her dream is opening a "open lab:darkroom"(darkroom+gallery+institution)for the community in future.
