Title: Have You Eaten? (P5JS)
Year: 2019

Description:
In Korea, asking "have you eaten?" is same concept as asking "how are you?"
If you did not have a meal yet, we start worry about you
and have a meal together or watch you to eat "something."
Have a meal, not skipping a meal is important in our culture.
I can tell this sentence, "Have you eaten?" represents our warm heart and caring about others.

Title: Exploring GridShape & Burning Birthday Cake
Year:2020

Description:
First one minute is me exploring gridshape on Max, and the last one minute is my work, “Burning Birthday Cake.”
I made a burning birthday cake using Blender, and I recorded myself playing the guitar “happy birthday to you” song.
I made string-like visuals in response to the guitar song.
Then, I connected that string-like visuals and my burning cake 3d object and glitch video together.
I can play backwards, and adjust the speed of the song as well.
It represents how I hope I would like to play my life backwards. Also, how much I do not want to get aged.
